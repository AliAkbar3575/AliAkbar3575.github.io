Research Interests

My research interests lie in the field of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer vision, Medical Imaging and Multimodal ML. I come from a developing country where the integration of advanced technologies in the medical sector remains limited. In addition, a large portion of population depends on agriculture that has minimal technological integration on farming. Therefore, this reality strongly motivated me to gain expertise in deep learning, computer vision in order to develop practical and impactful technological solutions for agriculture and healthcare. I am deeply interested in working in these research areas.

Nurse Care Activity Recognition from Accelerometer Sensor Data Using Fourier- and Wavelet-based Features

A series of pre-processing steps were performed, including data cleaning, resampling, windowing, and filtering.
